About Us

SUNGATE SOLTIONS LLC engages in designing, engineering, management, integration, construction, operation and maintenance of photovoltaic solar power and automation systems. Utilizing best practices of management, engineering, economics, health and safety in all aspects of its activities. SUNGATE SOLTIONS is currently registered in Union of Renewable Energy Industries in Palestine since 2014 with an official certificate from the union and Ministry of Economic. Through our experience and the expertise of our associated companies, partners and solar supporting equipment suppliers, we are able to give technical advice to our customers on design, engineering, installation and commissioning of photovoltaic systems, solar thermal system and Energy saving solutions for all types of projects.
